DISTRICT INDUSTRIES CENTER
THRISSUR

The 'District Industries Centre' (DICs) programme was started by the central government in 1978 with the objective of providing a focal point for promoting small, tiny, cottage and village industries in a particular area and to make available to them all necessary services and facilities at one place.

OUR VISION

To develop a district as a major industrial hub by developing globally competitive and quality product through MSMEs and employment opportunities may be provided through enhancing the quality industrial product at par with world standards & thereby contribute to the social, economical and professional growth & overall industrial development of the district Thrissur.

OUR MISSION

To indentify and encourage minimum 1000 new entrepreneurs to the field of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises and to develop minimum 700 enterprises providing 3500 employment opportunities and Rs. 110 Lakh investments in the district during 2015-16, which in turn provide contribution to the GDP and support for the sustainable development of the district.

Identify and develop places suitable for the infrastructure development such as DA/DP/Industrial Growth Centre for the SMEs as there is very much shortage of land for industrial purpose in Kerala. Developing existing DA and DPs with adequate and basic infrastructure facilities such as internal roads, power supply and water.

OUR SERVICES

  • IDENTIFICATION OF SCHEMES
  • PREPARATION OF FEASIBILITY REPORTS
  • ARRANGEMENT OF CREDIT FACILITIES
  • MACHINERY AND EQUIPMENTS
  • PROVISION FOR RAW MATERIALS
  • DEVELOPMENT OF INDUSTRIAL CLUSTERS

OUR HIERARCHY

Taluk industries offices (TIO) are the taluk level sub offices of DIC. There are five TIOs at taluk head quarters of Chavakkad, Kodungallur, Mukundapuram, Thalappally, Thrissur
