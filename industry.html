INDUSTRIAL PROFILE

Thrissur is said to be the heartland of Kerala's business acumen and home to most every leading entrepreneurs. It is famous for Bullion, Banking and its Business acumen, is the darling of investors in Kerala.
 
Thrissur is the headquarters of three scheduled banks in Kerala, South Indian Bank Ltd, Catholic Syrian Bank, and Dhanalakshmi Bank. The city also is the headquarters of Kerala State Financial Enterprise and leading non-banking finance companies in India. Thrissur's traditional strength lies in best entrepreneurial and financial capabilities.

Tile Industry

One of the significant industries of the area, the tile industry of Thrissur employs numerous labors from the state and outside. The Thrissur tile industry is more than a hundred years old.




Ayurveda Industry

Thrissur is also emerging as the largest hub for Ayurvedic drug manufacturing industry in the India. Out of the 850 ayurvedic drug-manufacturing units in Kerala, about 150 units, including some of the major ayurvedic drug manufacturers in the state are located in and around the Thrissur.

Gold and Diamond Jewellery Industry

Thrissur is referred as the Golden city of India. It manufactures 70% of plain gold jewellery in Kerala. The diamond production in South India is concentrated in Thrissur. Majority of the units located in Kapiparambu, Tholur, Adat, Choondal and Avanur.


Engineering and Machine Manufacturing

Mukudapuram and Thrissur taluks are famous for Engineering and Machine manufacturing units.








Plastic and allied Industry

The major manufacturers of PVC water pipes , Tanks,pipe fittings, Taps are located in the Region. Several Plastic manufacturing units are coming up in the District. As a supporting sector Tool and mold making facilities are also emerging.



Wooden Furniture

The wooden furniture industry is concentrated in Cherppu,Vallachira, Paralam region. Around 500 units are concentrated in this area.





Handloom Industry

There are 13 registered working Handloom Co-operative Societies in Thrissur District. They are Eravathody, Choondal Kondazhy, Thalikulam, Gandhi Memmorial, Kuthampully, Thiruvilwamala Anthikkad, Poyya, Nadavaramba, Cherpu, Irinjalakuda, Kodungallur HWCSS Limited. Kuthampully sarees is the product of Handloom sector of the District.
